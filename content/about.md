I'm **Hendra Manudinata** (usually called **Hendra**). I'm a student from Indonesia with
interests in web development, embedded devices, and cybersecurity. I'm currently pursuing a
bachelor's degree in [Information Technology](https://www.its.ac.id/it) at
[Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember](https://www.its.ac.id).

I tinker with Android, Linux, IoT, and embedded devices, but now I mostly work on web
applications. My [GitHub](https://github.com/manoedinata) and
[GitLab](https://gitlab.com/manoedinata) pages are where I store the majority of my work.
I usually make my projects FOSS and occasionally contribute to other open-source projects.

I'm also interested in robotics and embedded systems. I'm currently part of the programming
team at [IRIS ITS](https://iris.its.ac.id), a research & development team for wheeled robot
soccer competitions such as [FIRA RoboWorld Cup](https://firaworldcup.org),
[RoboCup](https://www.robocup.org), and Indonesia's
[Kontes Robot Indonesia](https://kontesrobotindonesia.id).
