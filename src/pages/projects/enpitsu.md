Enpitsu is a web/mobile-based assesment software. Enpitsu development was initially intended for SMAN 12 Bekasi to support the procurement of Computer-Based Test (CBT). Enpitsu is developed to fulfill the specific test requirements, providing a way to customize the internal mechanism of CBT.
