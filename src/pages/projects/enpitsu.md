## Background

Enpitsu is a web/mobile-based assesment software. Enpitsu development was initially intended for SMAN 12 Bekasi to support the procurement of Computer-Based Test (CBT).

To ensure smooth user access & neat deployment experience, an infrastructure design and supporting services are needed for Enpitsu. Thus, Enpitsu can be opened quickly, while facilitating the development & deployment process of Enpitsu itself.
